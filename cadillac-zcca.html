ZCCA Stand

Problem: Create an asset to communicate Cadillac’s forward-thinking future.
Insight: In order for people to fully understand Cadillac’s commitment to innovation and sustainability we want them to feel the full immersion of the experience.
Solution: A 3D printed stand made from recycled plastic with a VR experience showcasing the future of Cadillac.

Client: Cadillac
Status: Realised

Using innovative 3D printing from 100% recycle plastic, create a modern pod that houses a VR experience showcasing the Halo concept VTOL vehicle.

Execution

The shape came from the decision to keep the stand open on four sides for maximum accessibility, while keeping an organic shape that matches the futuristic designs of Cadillac and offering visitors shelter from the weather.
It was important to the client to have a reusable stand made of sustainable materials. This is where the idea to 3D print it out of 100% recycled plastic came from.
