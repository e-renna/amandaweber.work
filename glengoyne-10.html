10% Faster

Problem: Younger generations have become accustomed to a fast life-style and have lost the ability to slow down.
Insight: The pace of life has increased by 10% compared to thirty years ago.
Solution: Show that faster is not always better with an ad that is 10% faster.

Client: Glengoyne
Status: Idea

A radio ad that is 10% faster. Sounding rushed and difficult to understand, this would show that faster is not better and invite the listener to slow down in life.
