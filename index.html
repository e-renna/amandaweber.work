About Me

Hello! I am Amanda, a multinational Art Director/Hybrid.
I am a passionate communicator and creative problem-solver who is eager to start her journey in the world of Creative Advertising. With a background in photography, I have a curious eye that is fascinated by culture and people. I am also somebody who jumps in head first always on the lookout for an adventure. My ideas are guided by my structured way of working as well as my unique view that comes from growing up as a multinational in many different countries.

If you want to know more about me or my journey to where I am at now, I am happy to meet for a coffee, although I am more of a tea person ;)
