TRNSMT

Problem: With 975 festivals on offer in the UK, there is too much for people to choose from.
Insight: Young people go to festivals for the experience and culture.
Solution: Lean into TRNSMT’s unique Glaswegian festival culture by using the city to announce the line-up.

Client: TRNSMT
Status: Idea

Announce TRNSMT’s line-up using unique qualities of Glasgow, such as the cabbies and murals. This would share the Glaswegian festival culture, while involving the city and potentially attracting other communities to TRNSMT.

Teaming with Glasgow’s cabbies, TRNSMT Taxi Trivia gives citygoers the chance to test their music knowledge on the line-up to win tickets.
As cinch is TRNSMT’s biggest sponsor and they already do Taxi wraps, this would be easy to execute.

Discover the vibrant street art of Glasgow through a series of unique murals announcing the headliners, created by local Glaswegian artists.
Giving back to the city and its inhabitants, the street art would be enjoyable for everybody and potentially attract a slightly older audience to TRNSMT. It would also support local artists.
This would be executed as a treasure hunt over several days, with murals popping up throughout the city as excited festival goers look for the next announcement on the streets of Glasgow.
The locations would be chosen carefully to bring attention and people to certain areas of the city, for example neighbourhoods with shops that are struggling.
