Meat-free Meals on 2 Wheels

Problem: Get young people to make a conscious decision each time they eat.
Insight: Young people feel overwhelmed and powerless in the face of the climate crisis.
Solution: Give young people the chance to have an impact with their choice of takeaway food and delivery method.

Client: WWF
Status: Idea

By ordering a meat-free meal from Deliveroo and choosing cyclist as delivery option at check out, the service fee will go towards saving the endangered tree kangaroo.

Executions

WWF is partnering with Deliveroo to give young people a chance to make a difference by saving the endangered tree kangaroo.
When ordering a meat-free meal off the Deliveroo app, a pop-up will give the customer the chance to allow their service fee to go towards saving the endangered kangaroo. The only disadvantage being that they thereby consent to having their meal delivered by cyclist, meaning it might take a little longer.

The initiative would only run in areas where Deliveroo uses cyclists to deliver, such as in cities. It would be advertised in these areas using billboards.
